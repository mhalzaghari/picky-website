How it works

🎙️

Talk, don't type

Push-to-talk with a hotkey. Describe what you're working on or ask a specific question about your design. Picky listens and understands context.

👁️

It sees your screen

When you press the hotkey, Picky takes a screenshot and sends it to Claude AI for analysis. It understands layouts, spacing, typography, and color.

📌

Pins on your UI

Feedback isn't just text — Picky pins annotations directly onto the UI elements that need attention. Each pin is a floating label on your actual screen.

🖥️

Multi-monitor support

Works across all your connected displays. Picky maps coordinates to the right monitor and pins feedback exactly where it belongs.

🔒

Privacy first

Nothing runs in the background. Picky only captures your screen when you explicitly press the hotkey. No always-on recording.

⚡

Menu bar native

Lives in your menu bar, never in the dock. Lightweight, fast, and out of your way until you need it.
