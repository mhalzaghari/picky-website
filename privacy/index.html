Privacy Policy

Effective: April 15, 2026

What Picky Does

Picky is a macOS application that provides AI-powered design feedback. When you press the hotkey, Picky captures a screenshot of your screen and sends it along with your voice transcript to AI services for analysis.

Data We Collect

Picky collects minimal data:

  • Screenshots — Captured only when you explicitly press the hotkey. Never captured in the background.
  • Voice input — Recorded only during push-to-talk sessions. Not stored after transcription.
  • Basic analytics — Anonymous usage events (e.g., session started, feature used) to improve the product. No personal data is included.

Third-Party Services

Picky uses the following services to function:

  • Anthropic (Claude) — For AI-powered design analysis. Screenshots and transcripts are sent to Claude's API for processing.
  • AssemblyAI — For real-time voice transcription.
  • ElevenLabs — For text-to-speech playback of AI responses.
  • PostHog — For anonymous product analytics.

All API communication is routed through a secure proxy. No API keys are stored on your device.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not record your screen in the background.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising.
  • We do not store your screenshots or voice recordings after processing.

Data Storage

Picky does not maintain user accounts. Conversation history is stored locally on your Mac and is cleared when you quit the app. No personal data is stored on our servers.

Your Rights

Since we don't store personal data on our servers, there is no data to request deletion of. Your local conversation history is deleted when you quit the app.
